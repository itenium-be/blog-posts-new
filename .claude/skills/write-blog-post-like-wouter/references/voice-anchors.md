# Voice Anchors

Material to draw on (not check-boxes). Use what fits naturally; don't force.

## Phrase palette

Snark, self-deprecation, and dry asides:

- "Pretty neat!"
- "It's not my first rodeo"
- "Et voila"
- "Yaye!"
- "Uhoh"
- "How cool is X"
- "(gasp!)"
- "Pesky Windows."
- "Sure Claude, sure."
- "Yeah right."
- "Who has time for that?"
- "More AI to the rescue!"
- "I'm such an apologist…"
- "I'll spare you the details…"
- "Keep It Simple."
- "It depends" (linkable to the CodePen joke)
- "as always" / "of course" (when something is obvious in hindsight)
- "(just kidding:)" — appended after a provocative line
- "in true [TellDontAsk] style"
- "Yes please!"
- "Yet another thing I learned from…"

## Emoji palette

Use as inline punctuation/tone signal, not as bullet markers or decoration.

| Group               | Emojis                                       |
|---------------------|----------------------------------------------|
| Soft/playful        | `:)` `;)` `:D` `😀` `😉` `😎` `😅` `😃`      |
| Sheepish/wry        | `😇` `🙈` `😵`                                |
| Celebration         | `🎉` `🥳` `✨`                                |
| Intensity           | `🔥` `🔨` `💥`                                |
| Approval            | `👍` `⭐`                                     |

`✅` and `❌` are acceptable inside genuine pro/con lists, but not as the default bullet style.

## Belgian-English idioms (allowed)

These are signature, not errors — leave them in if they fit:

- "yaye" (instead of "yay")
- "Et voila"
- "2 gig" (instead of "2 gigabytes")

## Headers

- Title Case for the post `title` and most `## H2` section headings.
- Sentence case is OK for software-feature names (e.g. `## Conditional formatting`).
- Emoji in headers is fine: `## 🔥 The Regex`.

## Punctuation tics

- Em-dash `—` for parenthetical asides and quote attribution (`— Martin Fowler`).
- `* * *` (with spaces) for horizontal rules between major sections — never `---`.
- Multiple exclamation marks `!!` and bold combined for "no really, I mean it": `**never** fail`.
- Parentheticals like `(why put that first you ask…)` and `(gasp!)` welcome.
